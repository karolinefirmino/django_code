Research

Uberization, Gig Economy, Gig Work, Precarization, Informal Labor. All these terms, and a lot more, are used to describe in order to trying to grasp the complexity and heterogeneous phenomena in the currently organization of work in the global/digital market of today. How we can approach that? Legal frame of this question is enough? We believe not, but still, legal understand of these contemporary issues is essencial to think how to regulate these kind of works around the globe.
